You might think that learning how to play is simply a matter of learning the controls[^1]. In fact, the mods in MGO change most of Skyrim's systems to some degree&mdash;pretty dramatically in several cases&mdash;and you'll have more fun if you learn about these new/improved systems.

{{< chapter-heading img="movement-climb" >}}
## [Movement](movement)

Skyrim VR's vanilla movement options are still present, but MGO includes mods to enhance the experience of just getting around.

## [Interaction](interaction)

Why point at something and press <kbd>A</kbd> when you can bop it, twist it, pull it?
