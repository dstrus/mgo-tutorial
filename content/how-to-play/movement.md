Skyrim VR ships with the usual basic options for VR movement: Teleport vs. smooth motion, snap-turn vs. smooth turn. There's jumping and crouching/sneaking. All the usual stuff, and it's all done via thumbsticks. The game _is_ room-scale, so you can turn and move with your actual body (until you slam into furniture or a wall), but motion controls aren't involved beyond that.

If you've been paying any attention at all, you've probably guessed that Mad God's Overhaul includes mods that change that. {{< nexus 154803 >}}DovaVR Locomotion{{< /nexus >}} lets you move by swinging your arms, and {{< nexus 168553 >}}VR Climbing{{< /nexus >}} lets you climb any surface using your hands.
